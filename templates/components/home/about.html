Our Story

5th Avenue Grill and Restobar was established in Molave, Zamboanga del Sur with a passion for bringing authentic Filipino and international cuisine to our community. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a welcoming space where friends and families can gather to enjoy exceptional food and create lasting memories.

We take pride in our carefully crafted menu that showcases the rich flavors of Filipino cuisine alongside international favorites. Every dish is prepared with love, using traditional recipes and the freshest ingredients to ensure an unforgettable dining experience.
